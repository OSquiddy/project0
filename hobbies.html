TV Shows

Binge watching TV shows is always fun. It's how I'd seen Sherlock and Marvel's Daredevil for the first time. But there is something to be said about waiting for an episode to come out too. The anticipation is both something I hate and simultaneously love. I'd been following Game of Thrones since the third season. That's me watching a show over a period of 6 years. I absolutely loved it. Except for Season 8. That was the worst ending in the history of bad TV show endings.
